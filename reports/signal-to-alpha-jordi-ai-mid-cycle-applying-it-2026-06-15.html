From Discovery to DigestionIn macro, a mid-cycle slowdown is the pause inside an expansion. Growth is still positive and the cycle is still intact, but the rate of change cools, the early leaders get expensive, and investors trade an easy question for a harder one. The question shifts from “is this real?” to “what do I pay for the next dollar of it?” AI just ran through its early-cycle catch-up at a speed no normal economy could match. We went from skepticism to acceptance to a full capital rush in roughly six months. That catch-up is now behind us. What comes next looks a lot like a classic mid-cycle slowdown. The story is still powerful, but the easy money in the obvious trade is no longer sitting on the table.

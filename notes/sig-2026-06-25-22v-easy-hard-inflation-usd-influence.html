## Evidence Anchors

- 22V expects lower inflation as the economy respects a 2% speed limit, but the path matters more than the destination for equities.
- The easy path is slower GDP growth after the first-half fiscal boost, core inflation moving toward 3.3% YoY or lower, and the Fed staying on hold.
- The hard path is Fed hikes to slow GDP growth, raising recession probability and equity downside risk.
- Gold, 10-year yields, and breakevens speak to the destination of inflation; equities, curve shape, and credit spreads speak to the path.
- USD strength tightens financial conditions when it reflects rate differentials and higher short rates. 10-year yield declines and tight credit spreads currently offset some pressure.

HDetailed Appendix For Understanding

This appendix paraphrases the note and connects it to the existing research map.

The point is not that AI is over; the point is that surprise is lower.

The note argues that the agentic AI buildout remains early, but investors now understand more of the story. That means the market needs fresh proof, not just repeated confirmation that AI is real.

Agentic AI changes the infrastructure problem.

Agents are persistent systems that plan, reason, use tools, call models, and keep working. That creates demand for always-on inference, memory, networking, storage, power, cooling, and latency management.

The next phase is about expectations, timing, bottlenecks, and valuation.

Once the market has caught up to the story, stocks can fall even when the long-term technology is still improving. This is why the report should feed selectivity rules rather than broad AI buying.
